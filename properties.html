Preventive Care

Prevention is always better than cure>. Make sure to keep your pet in good health through comprehensive physical examination and health programs. It is also important to take note of advices given how to take care of your pet.


Emergency Care

When your pet is in dangerous situation, the clinic consider the appointment a special case. Call our telephone number under emergency situation. Talk to our vet doctor in duty to assess the situation of your pet while you're transporting your pet in our clinic. If the doctor assessed that the situation is needs to be immediately seen, the doctor might advice you to take your pet to the nearest veterinary clinic from your location. Emergency patients are always the top priority.


Surgery Services

We various kind of surgical procedures for your pets. Ranging from fracture repair, Skin abscesses or lacerations, Internal bleeding, Intestinal obstruction due to foreign body, Neuter, Dental extractions, and other common surgeries. Special Surgeries might not be available, pets will be recommended to other veterinary clinic to meet its needs.


Diagnostic Services

Determine underlying issues about the health of your pet through our Diagnostic services. We offer clinical, physical, laboratory and imaging diagnostic. Diagnostic services are helpful to detect health issues early, allowing the pet to be treated and received the best possible result.


Behavioral Counseling

Sometimes we might get confuse why our pets are acting weird as usual. There might be sudden change of behavior towards other animals and humans around. And there might be a situation that it already affects us and their health. Here comes behavioral counseling where Trainers (behavior counselor, pet psychologist or pet therapist) examine the behavior of your pets and help you on what to do to your pet.


Outpatient and Inpatient Clinic Care

Any illness your pet is diagnosed with can be treated at home, or in more serious cases, at our clinic. Outpatients are provided with medications from a well-stocked in-house pharmacy, while disabled patients are provided with 24-hour care, treatment and supervision by medical staff.


Avian and Exotic Pet Care

Dogs and Cats are the most common household pet but that doesn't mean that we do not care about other animals. Our team are dedicated to take care and give treatment to "non-traditional" pets such as birds and exotic pets.


Grooming and Boarding Services

Alongside of taking a good care of your pets health, you can also avail grooming services which can be a short or long term package. Boarding service is especially offered to busy pet parents where you can drop off your pets at clinic for the whole day. Slot may be limited, be sure to contact us first for reservation.


Online Consultation

Introducing our cutting-edge Online Veterinary Consultation Service, where pet owners can conveniently connect with our experienced veterinarians from the comfort of their homes. Our service offers easy scheduling, allowing users to book virtual consultations at their preferred times. Our skilled veterinarians conduct thorough assessments through video calls, addressing a range of health concerns and providing personalized care. Prescription services are available, with seamless delivery to the chosen pharmacy. Follow-up support ensures continuous monitoring, and our user-friendly platform prioritizes security and privacy. Experience the future of pet care with our Online Consultation Service, where expertise meets convenience, ensuring your pet's health is our top priority, anytime and anywhere.
