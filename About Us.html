OHANA SHED VETERINARY CLINIC

YOUR PET'S FAMILY SHED
Welcome to a place where every pet is treated like family. Experience dedicated veterinary care that ensures the well-being of your beloved extended family members. Established in 2030, Ohana Shed Veterinary Clinic was made to give shelter to your extended families, as Ohana from Hawaii means extended family. Our team have a dedication on taking care of your loving pets. Our clinic will be their shed whenever they are feeling discomfort.
Ohana Shed Veterinary Clinic is located along Bel-Air Village Phase II, 106 Jupiter, Makati, Metro Manila, and offers a complete range of medical, diagnostic and surgical services, and is equipped with the latest technological resources available. Pet grooming services are also available by appointment.
